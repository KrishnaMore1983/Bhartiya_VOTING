Frequently Asked Questions

What is the deadline for voter registration?

The deadline for voter registration is 30 days before the election date.

How can I check my voter registration status?

You can check your voter registration status by contacting your local election office.

Where do I find my polling place?

You can find your polling place by entering your address from our Find your polling place section.

What should I bring to the polling place?

Make sure to bring a valid photo ID and any other documentation required by your local election authority.
